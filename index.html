About Me

I am enthusiastic about full-stack web development and the endless opportunity for learning and growth that it provides me. I am a highly self-motivated person who constantly strives to be the best at what I am doing and will endlessly raise the bar for any challenge put in front of me. I find that solving new programming challenges or learning new languages and frameworks gives me a spark of excitement that naturally motivates me to keep pushing myself further. At the UT Coding Boot Camp, I have enjoyed working with teams and individually to bring new ideas to light. Making ideas into reality with my code is incredibly gratifying for me.


For the past few years I have been in the logistics industry which gave me data analysis, negotiation, and customer service experience. While working in that field, I placed a high importance on providing the best customer service experience possible to internal and external partners. I am the kind of person who likes to find solutions and answers as quickly and efficiently as possible, and I enjoy working in a fast paced environment that keeps me engaged.


My colleagues describe me as energetic and conscientious. My competencies are front and back-end JavaScript development, customer service, and consulting.


Skills and technologies: JavaScript, Node.js, HTML5, CSS3, Express.js, Sequelize, jQuery, MySQL, Bootstrap, D3.js, Responsive Design, Git, Firebase, RESTful API, AJAX, Heroku, Python, Ruby on Rails, Materialize
